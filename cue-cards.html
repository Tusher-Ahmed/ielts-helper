🎤 Part 1 — Predicted প্রশ্ন ব্যাংক + নমুনা Band-8 উত্তর

Part 1-এ পরিচিত/personal প্রশ্ন হয়। ফর্মুলা: সরাসরি উত্তর + কারণ/বিস্তার + ছোট উদাহরণ (২–৩ বাক্য)। এক শব্দে উত্তর দিও না।

💼 Work / Study

• Do you work or are you a student?   • What do you enjoy about it?   • Would you like to change it in future?

SAMPLE

"I'm currently working as a software engineer at a tech company, and I've been there for a couple of years now. What I enjoy most is that it's never monotonous — every project brings a fresh challenge, and I'm constantly picking up new skills. So yes, on the whole, it's a career I find genuinely rewarding."

🏙️ Hometown

• Where are you from?   • What's your hometown like?   • Has it changed much in recent years?

SAMPLE

"I was born and raised in Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh. It's an incredibly lively and crowded city, and while the traffic can be frustrating, it has a real charm. What I appreciate most is the strong sense of community — neighbours genuinely look out for one another."

🏠 Home / Accommodation

• Do you live in a house or a flat?   • What's your favourite room?   • Would you like to move in the future?

SAMPLE

"I live in a fairly modern flat with my family. My favourite spot is definitely the balcony — it gets plenty of light, and I love sitting there with a cup of tea after work. It's my little escape from a busy day."

⏰ Daily Routine

• What does a typical day look like for you?   • Are you a morning or an evening person?   • Has your routine changed recently?

SAMPLE

"To be honest, I'm very much an evening person. My days are quite structured around work, but I save my most productive hours for the evening, when it's quiet and I can focus. That's usually when I study or work on personal projects."

📱 Technology & Phones

• How often do you use your phone?   • What apps do you use most?   • Could you live without the internet?

SAMPLE

"Honestly, far more than I'd like to admit. My phone has become an extension of my hand. I mainly use it for staying in touch and reading the news, though I'm trying to cut down on mindless scrolling."

🍲 Food & Cooking

• What kind of food do you enjoy?   • Do you prefer eating at home or eating out?   • Can you cook?

SAMPLE

"I'm quite fond of traditional home-cooked food, especially rice and curry. I do enjoy eating out once in a while, but there's something comforting about a home-cooked meal. I can cook a few simple dishes, though I'd hardly call myself an expert."

🎵 Hobbies · Music · Weekends

💬 Part 3 — Discussion প্রশ্ন ব্যাংক + নমুনা Band-8 উত্তর

Part 3-এ abstract/opinion প্রশ্ন হয়। ফর্মুলা OREO: Opinion → Reason → Example → Opinion (restate)। উত্তর একটু লম্বা ও গোছানো হবে।

💻 Technology & Society

• Do you think technology will replace human jobs?   • Has technology made people more or less social?   • Why do some people resist new technology?

SAMPLE

"That's a thought-provoking question. Personally, I doubt technology will ever fully replace humans in most jobs. While machines are efficient, they lack the emotional intelligence and creativity people bring. For instance, a good teacher can sense when a student is struggling — something an algorithm arguably can't replicate. So, on balance, I believe technology will complement human work rather than replace it entirely."

🎓 Education & Learning

• Is it better to learn from a teacher or by yourself?   • How has the way people learn changed?   • Should education be free for everyone?

SAMPLE

"I'd argue that both have their place, but learning from a teacher is generally more effective, at least for beginners. A good teacher provides structure and immediate feedback, which self-study often lacks. That said, independent learning builds discipline and is invaluable later on. In the grand scheme of things, the ideal is a balance between the two."

💼 Work & Careers

• Is a high salary the most important thing in a job?   • Do you think people change jobs more than before?   • Will working from home become more common?

SAMPLE

"In my view, while salary obviously matters, it isn't the most important factor for most people. Job satisfaction and a healthy work-life balance tend to be far more fulfilling in the long run. For example, many talented people happily accept lower pay for work they find meaningful. So, ultimately, money alone rarely guarantees happiness at work."

🌍 Environment & Society

• Can individuals really help the environment?   • Whose responsibility is it to protect nature?   • Are people today more aware of environmental issues?

SAMPLE

"I firmly believe that individuals can make a real difference, even if it doesn't always seem that way. When millions of people reduce their carbon footprint and make greener choices, the combined effect is substantial. Moreover, ordinary people influence change indirectly, by pressuring companies and governments. So while big organisations bear the greatest responsibility, individual action is far from meaningless."

📢 Media & Advertising

• Does advertising influence what people buy?   • Is the news always reliable?   • How has social media changed the way we get information?

SAMPLE

"There's no doubt that advertising has a powerful influence on our choices, often without us realising it. Advertisers cleverly associate products with positive feelings rather than facts. For instance, many people buy a particular brand simply because of the image it projects. On the whole, I think being aware of these techniques helps us make more rational decisions."

🌐 Culture & Tradition

• Is it important to preserve traditions?   • Does globalisation threaten local cultures?   • How can young people be encouraged to value their heritage?

SAMPLE

"To a certain extent, I agree that globalisation poses a risk to local cultures, as international trends can overshadow traditional customs. However, I believe it also enriches cultures by encouraging exchange. A good example is how traditional food and music can now reach a global audience. The key, in my view, is to embrace the benefits while consciously preserving what makes each culture unique."
