🪜 প্রতিটা এসের ৪-ধাপ পদ্ধতি

  1. Analyse (১ মিনিট): প্রশ্নের ধরন চেনো, keyword চিহ্নিত করো, ঠিক কী চাইছে বোঝো।
  2. Plan (৪ মিনিট): অবস্থান ঠিক করো + ২টা মূল আইডিয়া + প্রতিটার উদাহরণ নোট করো।
  3. Write (৩০ মিনিট): ৪ প্যারা — Intro, Body 1, Body 2, Conclusion।
  4. Check (৫ মিনিট): গ্রামার, বানান, শব্দ সংখ্যা, article/preposition রিভিউ।

📝 Task 2 — Essay (সবচেয়ে গুরুত্বপূর্ণ)

৫টি প্রশ্নের ধরন। প্রথমেই ধরন চেনা মানে অর্ধেক কাজ শেষ।

৫টি প্রশ্নের ধরন ও তাদের স্ট্রাকচার

ধরনপ্রশ্নে যা থাকেস্ট্রাকচার
Opinion (Agree/Disagree)"To what extent do you agree?"একপক্ষ নাও; ২ body-তেই সেটা সমর্থন

🏗️ ৪-প্যারা কাঠামো — প্রতিটা প্যারা কীভাবে লিখবে

1️⃣ Introduction (২ বাক্য)

বাক্য ১ — Paraphrase: প্রশ্নটা নিজের ভাষায় লেখো।
বাক্য ২ — Thesis: তোমার অবস্থান + এসে কী বলবে তার ইঙ্গিত।

"It is often argued that [topic paraphrased]. This essay will argue that [your position], as [reason 1] and [reason 2]."

2️⃣ + 3️⃣ Body Paragraphs — TEE ফর্মুলা

T — Topic sentence (প্যারার মূল আইডিয়া)
E — Explain (কেন? বিস্তারিত ব্যাখ্যা)
E — Example (বাস্তব/বিশ্বাসযোগ্য উদাহরণ)

To begin with, [topic sentence]. This is because [explanation]. For instance, [specific example]. As a result, [mini-conclusion linking back]."

4️⃣ Conclusion (১–২ বাক্য)

"In conclusion" + মতামত restate + কোনো নতুন আইডিয়া না।

"In conclusion, while [acknowledge other side], I firmly believe that [restate position] because [summary of reasons]."

💡 আইডিয়া বের করার কৌশল (blank মাথা এড়াও)

  • Ask "why?" & "example?": প্রতিটা দাবির পেছনে কারণ ও একটা উদাহরণ খোঁজো।
  • Categories: ভাবো — economic, social, health, environmental, educational দিক থেকে।
  • Stakeholders: কারা প্রভাবিত হয়? — individuals, government, businesses, students।
  • উদাহরণ বানানো যায়: "A recent study suggests…" / "In many countries…" — নির্দিষ্ট হলেই ভালো।
⚠️ Task 2 এর কমন ভুল: প্রশ্নের একাংশ বাদ দেওয়া • অবস্থান অস্পষ্ট রাখা • এক প্যারায় একাধিক আইডিয়া • memorised phrase ঠেসে দেওয়া • ২৫০ শব্দের কম লেখা • উপসংহারে নতুন আইডিয়া।

📊 Task 1 (Academic) — Data বর্ণনা

সব ধরনের chart-এ একই তিন-ভাগ কাঠামো: Intro → Overview → Details। মতামত দিও না।

🏗️ সর্বজনীন কাঠামো

  1. Introduction (১ বাক্য): প্রশ্নটা paraphrase করো — chart কী দেখাচ্ছে।
  2. Overview (২ বাক্য): সবচেয়ে গুরুত্বপূর্ণ ২–৩টি ট্রেন্ড/বৈশিষ্ট্য (সংখ্যা ছাড়া)। এটাই সবচেয়ে বেশি নম্বর টানে।
  3. Body 1 & 2: নির্দিষ্ট সংখ্যা/ডেটা দিয়ে বিস্তারিত, যুক্তিসঙ্গতভাবে গ্রুপ করে।
✅ Overview-তে "Overall," দিয়ে শুরু করো — examiner সরাসরি এটা খোঁজে।

প্রতিটা chart type কীভাবে ধরবে

📈 Line graph (সময়ের সাথে পরিবর্তন)

Trend ভাষা ব্যবহার করো: rose, fell, peaked, fluctuated। সময় গুরুত্বপূর্ণ।

Overview: Overall, [X] rose steadily over the period, whereas [Y] showed a downward trend, with both converging by the end.

📊 Bar chart (তুলনা)

Comparison ভাষা: higher than, the highest, twice as many as, compared with।

Overview: Overall, [category] had by far the highest figure, while [category] accounted for the smallest proportion.

🥧 Pie chart (অনুপাত)

Proportion ভাষা: accounts for, makes up, a quarter, the majority, a fraction।

Detail: [X] made up nearly half of the total, whereas [Y] accounted for just under a fifth.

📋 Table (ডেটা)

সব ডেটা লিখো না — সবচেয়ে বড়, ছোট ও লক্ষণীয় বেছে নাও। গ্রুপ করে তুলনা করো।

⚙️ Process diagram

Overview = কতগুলো ধাপ + শুরু-শেষ কোথায়। Passive + sequence শব্দ: First, Next, After that, Finally, is then + V3।

First, the raw material is collected. It is then processed, after which it is packaged for delivery.

🗺️ Map (পরিবর্তন)

Location ও change ভাষা: to the north of, was replaced by, was demolished, a new X was built। Past passive যদি অতীত হয়।

The forest to the east was cleared and replaced by a residential area.

Task 2 — Opinion (Agree/Disagree)

প্রশ্ন: Some people believe that children should be taught how to manage money at school. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

BAND 8 MODEL

It is increasingly argued that financial literacy should form part of the school curriculum. I completely agree with this view, as teaching children to manage money equips them with an essential life skill and helps prevent future debt.

To begin with, money management is a practical skill used throughout life. Unlike many academic subjects, budgeting and saving are applied daily. For instance, a child who learns to save part of their pocket money is far more likely to develop responsible financial habits as an adult. Consequently, schools that overlook this leave students unprepared for the real world.

Furthermore, early financial education can significantly reduce the risk of debt. Many young adults struggle financially simply because they were never taught how credit works. A clear example is the rising number of university students burdened with loans they barely understand. Had they been educated earlier, such problems could largely be avoided.

In conclusion, I strongly believe that schools should teach money management, since it is a vital skill that fosters responsibility and protects young people from future hardship.

Task 2 — Discussion (Both views)

প্রশ্ন: Some people prefer to work for a large company, while others prefer a small one. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

BAND 8 MODEL

Choosing between a large corporation and a small firm is a decision many job-seekers face. While both have clear merits, I personally believe that small companies offer greater long-term growth.

On the one hand, large companies provide notable advantages, chiefly job security and structured career paths. Employees often enjoy better salaries and training. For example, multinational firms typically offer clear promotion ladders, which appeals to those seeking stability.

On the other hand, smaller companies allow individuals to take on diverse responsibilities and see the direct impact of their work. As a software engineer, I have found that in a smaller team, one acquires new skills far more quickly. This hands-on exposure, in my view, is invaluable early in a career.

In conclusion, although large firms offer security, I believe the varied experience gained in a small company ultimately benefits professional development more.

Task 2 — Problem / Solution

প্রশ্ন: Traffic congestion is a serious problem in many cities. What are the causes, and what measures can solve it?

BAND 8 MODEL

Traffic congestion has become a pressing issue in urban areas worldwide. This essay will examine its main causes and propose realistic solutions.

The primary cause is the sheer rise in private car ownership. As incomes grow, more people buy vehicles, putting a strain on roads never designed for such volumes. In addition, inadequate public transport forces commuters to rely on cars, worsening the problem.

To tackle this, governments should invest heavily in efficient public transport such as metro systems, making it a genuine alternative to driving. A further measure is congestion charging, which has proven effective in cities like London by discouraging unnecessary car use. As a result, both traffic and pollution decline.

In conclusion, congestion stems largely from rising car use and weak public transport, but with sustained investment and smart policies, it can be substantially reduced.

Task 1 — Line graph (full model)

প্রশ্ন: The graph shows the percentage of households with internet access in three countries between 2000 and 2020.

BAND 8 MODEL

The line graph illustrates the proportion of homes with internet access in three countries over a two-decade period from 2000 to 2020.

Overall, internet access rose dramatically in all three nations, with Country A consistently leading, while the figures converged at high levels by the end of the period.

In 2000, only around 20% of households in Country A had access, compared with a mere 5% in Country C. Over the following years, access in Country A climbed steadily, peaking at roughly 95% in 2020. Similarly, Country C witnessed the sharpest growth, surging from 5% to almost 90% over the same period, thereby narrowing the gap considerably.

🔤 Grammar & Vocabulary (Writing-এর জন্য)

🧩 যে ৫টি Complex বাক্য গঠন ব্যবহার করবে

  • Conditional: If governments invested more, pollution would decline.
  • Relative clause: Students who manage their time well tend to succeed.
  • Concession: Although technology is useful, it has drawbacks.
  • Cleft: It is education that drives long-term change.
  • Inversion: Not only does it save money, but it also saves time.
